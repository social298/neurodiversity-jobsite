FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly will my job go live? +
Network Package listings go live within 1 hour across all 9 boards. Single listings are reviewed for inclusive language first and typically go live within 1 business day.
What exactly is the AI candidate matching? +
Our AI analyses your vacancy — the role requirements, work environment, and accommodations offered — and matches it to neurodivergent candidates based on skills, strengths, and preferences. Not just keywords. Better candidates, faster hires.
What is the governance evidence report? +
A date-stamped PDF proving your organisation actively reached neurodivergent candidates before shortlisting. With employment tribunals rising and board-level scrutiny increasing, this protects your organisation and satisfies Equality Act 2010 obligations. Our AI generates it automatically.
We already have a D&I strategy — why do we need this? +
A strategy without evidence is a liability. 76% of neurodivergent employees haven't disclosed their condition — meaning your workforce data underrepresents the problem. We give you documented, defensible proof of action at every vacancy level.
Can I upgrade after posting? +
Yes. Email sales@diversityjobsite.co.uk and we'll upgrade you immediately. Cost difference is applied as a credit.
Is there a minimum commitment for the Unlimited plan? +
