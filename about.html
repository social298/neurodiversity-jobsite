Neurodiversity Jobsite is the UK's specialist neurodivergent job board — part of the Diversity Jobsite Network, connecting inclusive employers with the talent that mainstream hiring overlooks.

Who We Are

Part of the Diversity Jobsite Network

Neurodiversity Jobsite is the newest specialist board within the Diversity Jobsite Network — the UK's leading network of diversity-focused job boards, trusted by over 400 organisations including the NHS, EY, UK Parliament, and the Financial Conduct Authority.

The network has been operating for 18 years, giving us deep expertise in specialist hiring, inclusive employer vetting, and diversity outreach. Across our 9 boards, we attract over 200,000 monthly visitors from candidates actively seeking employers who take inclusion seriously.

Neurodiversity Jobsite was created to address a specific and urgent gap: despite 10 million neurodivergent people in the UK, the unemployment rate among neurodivergent adults sits at 70%. Mainstream job boards were not designed for them. We were.

Our Mission

To connect every neurodivergent professional in the UK with an employer who genuinely values what they bring.

We believe that ADHD, autism, dyslexia, dyspraxia, and other neurodivergent conditions are not barriers to employment — they are sources of exceptional talent. Pattern recognition, hyperfocus, creative problem-solving, and lateral thinking are skills that high-performing organisations need. Our mission is to make the match happen, at scale, with the evidence employers need to prove their commitment.
