Neurodiversity Jobsite exists to make sure UK employers are on the right side of that line — and that the 10 million neurodivergent adults in this country get the careers they deserve.

What We're Building

Not a job board. An intelligence layer for neurodivergent hiring.

A standard job board lists vacancies. We're building the operating system for UK neurodivergent careers — four tightly integrated products, one proprietary data layer, zero compromise on outcomes.

PILLAR 01

Strengths-First Candidate Intelligence

We replace the CV — a format systemically biased against neurodivergent careers — with a structured strengths and accommodations profile. Candidates are matched to roles on pattern recognition, hyperfocus domains, communication style and sensory preferences, not on keyword density or gaps in a work history.

PILLAR 02

AI Job Description Accessibility Engine

Every JD posted is automatically scored, rewritten and certified for cognitive accessibility — removing exclusionary language, clarifying expectations, signalling accommodations. Employers get instantly more applicants. Candidates get roles written in a language they can actually decode.

PILLAR 03

Pay Gap & Compliance Evidence Suite

Built for the March 2026 reporting regime: automated disability pay gap calculations, action plan generators, PSED audit trails, tribunal defensibility evidence, and governance exports. The legal team's favourite tool, bundled into recruitment.

PILLAR 04

The UK Employer Neurodiversity Index

A public, verifiable score for every UK employer based on policy, pay gap, tribunal history and candidate-reported experience. Turns inclusion from a claim into a benchmark — and gives serious employers a reason to outperform.

Proprietary Intelligence

The only dataset of its kind in the UK market.

Every search, every application, every accommodation request, every successful placement on our platform becomes structured signal — fed into the only live dataset of UK neurodivergent hiring behaviour in existence.

After twelve months, this data layer doesn't just power better matching — it becomes the single most valuable strategic asset in the UK diversity hiring industry. It tells employers which accommodations actually drive retention. It tells candidates which employers actually follow through. It tells regulators which interventions actually work.

General-purpose AI platforms can't replicate this. They weren't there for the last 18 years. They won't be there for the next five.
